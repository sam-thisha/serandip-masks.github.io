Splash Screen.

I used HTML, CSS, and Javascript to create a splash screen. I made an effort to make it more appealing. I've therefore incorporated a video background. That video background was made by me. And for the animations, I utilized Javascript. Our theme is traditional masks. So I tried my best to add a traditional look to our splash page.

Buy Products.

The creation of a Purchase Items page is my second task. Our website is based on traditional masks from Sri Lanka. Therefore I made the decision to introduce categories for wooden masks and merchandise based on old-fashioned masks. Items designated "Yaka Face" are highly sought after because of Sri Lankan mask art's enormous tourist appeal. I consequently uploaded those items to our website.Moreover, our online store offers products in 8 categories, and our buyers have the option to select an additional 4 items from each category based on buyers preferences.
With our website, we have provided our customers with the ability to view the images of the products we sell in all categories as well as their accurate names. (When you click on that name, you can also get the related image.) Customers can search up information about things they previously expected to purchase as well as information about new items they have added to their shopping cart at any moment. Customers are also given the option to remove an item they have previously added to the cart.
The cart will ask for the name and phone number of the respective buyer and that information will be automatically entered at the checkout.

Events Page.
